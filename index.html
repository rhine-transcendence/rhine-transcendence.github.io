Rhine Seminar on Transcendence Basel-Freiburg-Strasbourg

The rotating seminar is a two, one day event per year between the three cities. For more information you can contact the organisers of the different clusters, namely Giuseppe Ancona and Thomas Dreyfus (Strasbourg), Amador Martin-Pizarro and Annette Huber (Freiburg), Philipp Habegger (Basel)
